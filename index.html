Self-Introduction

I graduated from Singapore Polytechnic with a Diploma in Games Design and Development. My time in Polytechnic had given me the opportunity to work on 2D and 3D game projects, helping me pick up software such as Photoshop, 3DS Max, and ZBrush.

I enjoy cycling, practicing the electric Bass, and playing video games in my free time.
